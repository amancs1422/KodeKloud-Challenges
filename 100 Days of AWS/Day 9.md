# Day 9 : Enable Termination Protection for EC2 Instance
## Step 1 : Access the EC2 dashboard in the AWS Management Console.
![](https://github.com/amancs1422/KodeKloud-Challenges/blob/8a66f82fe925aea54d5dbc71b305adff88f4783c/100%20Days%20of%20AWS/Images/Day%209_1.jpg)
## Step 2 : Find the instance as per the lab requirements.

## Step 3 : Select the instance and choose the "Change termination protection" option.
![](https://github.com/amancs1422/KodeKloud-Challenges/blob/8a66f82fe925aea54d5dbc71b305adff88f4783c/100%20Days%20of%20AWS/Images/Day%209_3.jpg)
## Step 4 : Check mark the enable option and hit "Save".
